About:

Image Opus is a website setup to explain and show what image formats work on every modern day browser and how they function. In this website there will be a tutorial explaining image formats with a description and sample of each format. The file formats are JPEG, PNG and GIF and this can be found in the tutorial page.


In the contact page you will find a form that you can fill out and send to us with suggestions for the site, complaints or any queries you may have. I hope you enjoy your visit to our site.
